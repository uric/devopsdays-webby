**Abstract:**

A typical sysadmin day with ralph.

Ralph is full-featured Asset Management, DCIM and CMDB system for data center and back office.

And what a typical day working with the Ralph looks like? Answers to this question will know from the point of view of the sysadmin. Will be presented daily tasks, eg. adding a new machine/device to the system Ralph, updated information about the detected hardware and software, etc. Tasks with which you may encounter in sysadmin everyday life.

**Speaker:**

Daniel Pijanowski works in Allegro Group as a System Administrator. From the beginning he is related with building and maintaining the infrastructure shared services within Allegro Group.
