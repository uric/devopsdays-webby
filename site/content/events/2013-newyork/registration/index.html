Registration is open!

Free t-shirts are a tradition at most tech conferences. But then again, devopsdays is not like most conferences! So this year, instead printing yet another conference t-shirt, devopsdays NYC is donating $12 of your registration fee to the non-profit Lotus Outreach. With this money, we will build a deep-bore water well that will provide life-saving clean, safe drinking water to approximately 200 rural villagers in Cambodia for years to come. To learn more about Lotus Outreach and this program, please see this video.
