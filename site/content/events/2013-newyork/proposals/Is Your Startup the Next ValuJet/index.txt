**Abstract:**

Most people recognize ValuJet as the airline that closed years ago after a number of high-profile incidents and accidents. This Ignite talk will look at the culture that set the stage for those incidents and--surprisingly--how those lessons may be forewarnings to smaller organizations building businesses on cloud-based technologies.
