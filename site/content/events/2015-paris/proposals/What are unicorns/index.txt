**Abstract:**

We all know what unicorns are but in the DevOps world it does hold a special meaning; I's all about those crazy companies, mostly start-ups which have embraced DevOps early on and are now doing it right, from A to Z.

**Speaker:**

*Bertrand Besnard*

Bertrand Besnard is a French Software Engineer who migrated to Oslo. He has since then been working on some of the biggest Norwegian projects as a DevOps such as NAV and Autosys. He is currently employed by Sopra Steria.
He is interested in process analysis and human interactions and travels the world to discover more cultures, and food, lots of food.
