**Abstract:**

In this talk, we'll explain how we maintain our internal OpenStack clouds and some OpenStack clouds for customers inside the Red Hat Cloud Innovation Practice. We'll describe our CI/CD methodology and tools. How devs and ops collaborate to adapt and deploy OpenStack for customers' specific needs.


**Speaker:**

*Frédéric Lepied*

Frederic Lepied has been involved in the Free Software/OpenSource movement since 1996. He has contributed to different projects like Mandriva Linux automating the build of this Linux distribution (rpm-rebuilder), the quality assurance of packages (rpmlint), the validation of aLinux distribution regarding hardware (testzilla), CI bot for webkit at Cisco/NDS, automation tools to deploy OpenStack on baremetal and virtual environments at eNovance/Red Hat (SpinalStack).
