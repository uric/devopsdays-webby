**Abstract:**

This talk will discuss the processes and practices that organizations can use to improve their security demeanor when applying change to systems in a fast moving world.  It explores ways in which compliance can be applied and controlled at velocity.  By having the correct processes and controls in place early in the delivery life cycles you can minimize your exposure and risk to security.  It will also highlight ways to manage your audit and compliance requirements whilst being able to resolve issues in a fast and controlled manner.
