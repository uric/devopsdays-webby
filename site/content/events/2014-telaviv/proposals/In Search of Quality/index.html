--- extension: html filter: - erb - markdown dirty: true proposal: true talk: true selected: false layout: event author: Yossef Mendelssohn title: "In Search of Quality" --- **Abstract:** Through the ages, people have struggled to define Quality. It can be as lofty as a discussion of character, of excellence, of a superior way of every aspect coming together in elegant balance — or it can be just about something being good or bad. To put it simply, Quality is an imprecise concept. **Speakers:** Yossef's been working with computers for most of his life. What he cares about now is people — helping them work together, helping them learn together, helping them build great things together. Helping them make the world a better place. Oh, and dancing. But isn't that the same thing, really?
