**Abstract**

An almost serious introduction to DevOps from an operational point of view. What DevOps is about and how we can promote DevOps practices and culture in a company.
We will review the principles, the tools, the methodologies, the patterns and the culture and we will see how we they can be introduced and embraced in our working place.

Target audience: The talk is oriented to who is relatively new to DevOps or needs to have a wider and more complete vision on what and how is all about.

**Speaker**

Alessandro Franceschi, Author or the book "Extending Puppet", and something else.
