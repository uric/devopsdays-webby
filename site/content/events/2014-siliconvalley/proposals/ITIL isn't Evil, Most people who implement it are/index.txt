**Abstract:**

ITIL isn’t evil, most people who implement ITIL don’t execute on the Continuos Improvement process. 

DevOps and ITIL (and ITSM) actually work together very well. In this talk will with highlight the change 
management and continuous improvement process area’s in ITIL and ITSM and discuss how implement 
DevOps methodologies can *increase* the level of compliance and service of a classic IT organization.

* Why DevOps and ITIL go well together
	** DevOps in shadow IT, reaction to lack of IT services for developers
	** ITIL Service Creation 
	** ITIL Continuous Improvement
	** DevOps Continuous Improvement 
	** ITILv3 and DevOps Aligned Area’s

* ITILv3 change and release management process and DevOps toolchains
	** DevOps Maturity levels and Improvement area’s
	** Level 0 Improvements, and mapping into ITIL Area’s
	** Level 1 Improvements, and mapping into ITIL Area’s
	** Level 2 Improvements, and mapping into ITIL Area’s

* Suggested next steps
	** Technology
	** Process
	** Organizational Transformation
