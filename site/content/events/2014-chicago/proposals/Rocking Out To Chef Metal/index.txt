**Abstract**

Chef recently announced Chef Metal, a framework for managing clusters of machines in the same way you traditionally managed single nodes: with version-controlled recipes. Join us as we walk through how Rackspace uses Chef Metal and provisioners in a live demo deployment on an OpenStack cloud.
