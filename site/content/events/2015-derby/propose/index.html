Speaker selection

The selection is done by the [complete organization team](../contact) over a discussion. We take into account comments/remarks at the bottom of each proposal. Once selection is complete, we will notify both selected and non selected speakers. Optionally we will tell people they are a runner up or ask them to propose the same subject as an Ignite talk.
