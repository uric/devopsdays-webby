title: "Loadbalancers as a Feature Switch"

**Abstract:**

If your application is advertising itself into a service discovery mechanism for your load balancer to consume, can this mechanism be combined with your source control system to provide a generic feature switch will doesn't concern the internals of your application?

This lightning talk will introduce a small example of how this might be achieved.

Duncan Ward
